Banana bread

Bananas!

Who doesn't love the yellow fruit?

This time let's make bread out of it

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flout
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 deg. celcius
  2. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan
  3. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended
  5. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture, pour batter into prepared loaf pan
  6. Bake in oven for 60 minutes
  7. Let it cool and bon apetit!
