Tomato soup

Fresh and tasty tomato soup, just as your mom used to make it

This soup is so extraordinary, you will spend all your pennies on tomatoes after you eat just a spoonful

After preparing this recipe, you will never want to go back to canned soup!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 slice onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugra, or to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic cloves and chicken broth about 20 minutes
  2. Run the mixture trough a food mill into a large bowl
  3. In original stockpot mix melted butter and flour, to create a roux
  4. And finally... gradually add tomato mixture to the stockpot. Remember to whisk continuously so no lumps form
