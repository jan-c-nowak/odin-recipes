Sweet Potato Muffins

Sweet and tasty!

Do you like traditional muffins?

I can guarantee that you will love ones made with sweet potatos!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup cooked, mashed sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 and 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 deg. celcius
  2. Grease a 12-cup muffin pan
  3. Combine sugar, sweet potato, olive oild, water, and eggs in a bowl
  4. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a second bowl
  5. Add flour mixture and pecans to the sweet potato mixture
  6. Mix, be careful not to overmix
  7. Spoon batter into the muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full
  8. Bake until a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean, 20 minutes
