French toast

Bonjour! Time to prepare sweet and delicious breakfast

Simple and yet delicious, this recipe could be easily prepared by a 9-year old

Grab you coffee makers and prepare your tongues to break from the excessive use of french accent, let's go!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and salt together in a bowl
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side
