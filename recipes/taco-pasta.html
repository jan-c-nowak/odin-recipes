Taco pasta

For all the italo-taco lovers

If you ever find yourself with taco leftovers, this is the meal to go

Fairly simple, and yet amazing. These words sum up this dish perfectly

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1.25 ounce package taco seasoning mix
  • 4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 6 ounce can black olives, drained and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta al dente
  2. Meanwhile, cook ground beef in a large skillet
  3. Drain excess grease and add taco seasoning as instructed on the packet
  4. Put pasta in a large bowl and toss with cooked meat, prepared cheese and tomatoes
  5. Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder
  6. Sprinkle with parmesan. Add olives if desired
